How to Play Boom! Game

Boom! is an explosive turn-based game where players take turns to outsmart each other using a variety of weapons and power-ups on destructible battlefields.

Controls

Use your left mouse button to interact with the game UI. Drag and aim using the on-screen controls.

Movement

Drag the stick to aim your jump direction, then release to leap across the battlefield. You can jump twice per turn!

Attack

Choose your weapon (bazooka, gun, poop...), aim with the stick, and shoot! Time your shots carefully to avoid obstacles.

Power-ups

Collect and activate special power-ups during your turn to gain advantages over your opponents.

How Boom! Game Works

Boom! operates on a turn-based combat system where players take alternating turns to move, attack, and use items. Each turn consists of several phases that create strategic depth and exciting gameplay. The goal is to eliminate all opponents using skillful aiming, clever movement, and tactical use of the environment.

1

Turn Begins

Your turn starts with a full jump charge and access to all available actions.

2

Movement Phase

Jump around the battlefield to position yourself strategically.

3

Attack Phase

Aim and fire your weapon at opponents or the environment.

4

Turn Ends

Your turn concludes and the next player takes their actions.
